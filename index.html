HOW IT WORKS


CAN CTC REALLY "RESTORE" WATER TO DEWATERED STREAMS,
RIVERS AND WETLANDS IN THE COLORDO RIVER BASIN?

range of projects that help restore water flow. Want to know how its done?


H2O CONSERVATION

Change the Course funds projects that invest in new irrication infustructure allowing farmers to conserve and or divert less water from dewatered regions.

We help upgrade outdated water delivery systems, helping irrigators divert only the nessesary water supply for their aggriculure and irrigation needs.
